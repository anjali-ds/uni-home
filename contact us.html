The University of ADS is a premier university of the country with a venerable legacy and international acclaim for highest academic standards, diverse educational programmes, distinguished faculty, illustrious alumni, varied co-curricular activities and modern infrastructure.

Established in 1921 as a unitary, teaching and residential University by the Act of the then Central Legislative Assembly, a strong commitment to excellence in teaching, research.
Six Departments namely ENGINEERING, MANAGEMENT, HUMANITIES, PHARMACY, LAW and SCIENCE have been awarded the status of the Centres of Advanced Studies.
